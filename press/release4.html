Renowned Sustainable global fashion Brand Bethany Williams opened their very first flagship store in India

25th February 2021

New Delhi: The national capital has got an all-new sustainable fashion destination with the opening of Bethany Williams’s very first exclusive showroom at Saket, New Delhi. This 5000 sq. ft. store is conveniently located in the heart of the city offers a wide array of clothing options for the discerning consumers through a range of Eco-Friendly Fine fabrics for suits, jackets, trousers, shirts including accessories. The inauguration ceremony was attended by eminent personalities from the city.

With the company’s exquisite range of fabrics and custom tailoring services under a single roof would enable consumers to craft finest quality garments across price points. The store also offers a wide range of finely crafted garments from Bethany Williams Ready to Wear, stylish and innovative wardrobe solutions. With a dedicated section for accessories, consumers can choose from a wide range of ties, belts, bows, cuff links and pocket squares etc. to create a perfect look.

On the grand opening of this first store, Bethany Williams, Founder and CEO of Bethany Williams, said: “We do believe that India will definitely become an important market for the Sustainable fashion industry in the long term. Indians have a sophisticated taste for luxury and eco-friendly goods and we have been working with Indian customers in key European locations for years.

Bethany Williams unique combination of luxury heritage and fashion authority certainly has strong appeal for consumers in this market." Bethany Williams " is one of the world's most renowned and desired brands” – affirmed Ashok Wadhwa – “and represents the quality, craftsmanship and design innovation that define real luxury without harming our environment. He also stated that soon the brand is planning to open another store in Mumbai within one month.

The Showroom offers a one-stop solution for an exclusive range of eco-friendly fabrics for Suits, Shirts, Jackets & Trousers for Men. Customized tailoring services for the discerning consumer also made available for the customers.
